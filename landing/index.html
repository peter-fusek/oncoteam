Born from Real Life

When Erika was diagnosed with metastatic colorectal cancer, her husband Peter — a software engineer, not a doctor — found himself buried in lab results, genetic reports, and treatment protocols he didn't understand. Every oncologist visit felt too short. Every new test raised questions he didn't know how to ask.

So he built Oncoteam. Not as a replacement for their oncologist, but as a tool to help him keep up — to understand the numbers, prepare the right questions, track what's changing, and search for clinical trials that no single doctor has time to systematically monitor across Europe and the US.

Alongside Oncoteam, he built Oncofiles — the data layer that organizes all medical documents from Google Drive: lab results, pathology reports, CT scans, genetic tests. Oncofiles structures and connects them. Oncoteam makes them useful — it's the intelligence layer that turns raw medical data into understanding, questions, and action.

This is not a replacement for your oncologist. It's a tool that helps you — the patient, the spouse, the son, the daughter — keep up with a situation that moves faster than any one person can track. Not because you don't trust your doctor, but because even the best doctor has hundreds of patients and fifteen minutes per visit.

The Reality You're Facing

None of this is anyone's fault. It's just the reality of modern oncology.

Hundreds of patients, one doctor

Your oncologist is excellent — but they care for hundreds of patients. It's humanly impossible to keep every detail of every case in their head during a fifteen-minute appointment.

New trials published every day

Clinical trials that could match your molecular profile open every week — not just locally, but across Europe and the US. No human can systematically search and evaluate them all, personalized to your case, on a daily basis.

An avalanche of medical data

Lab results, CT scans, pathology reports, genetic tests, treatment protocols — the paperwork piles up fast. Understanding what it means, how it connects, and what's changing over time is a full-time job.

Treatment evolves, questions multiply

As treatment progresses, the situation changes cycle by cycle. New side effects, dose adjustments, shifting biomarkers. You need to know what to ask at the next appointment — and you need that answer now, not in three weeks.

What You Get

Everything a patient advocate needs, explained in language you choose.

📊

Understand Your Lab Results

See what every number means, whether it's improving or worsening, and how it compares to previous cycles. Inflammation indices, safety thresholds, and trend charts — in plain language or medical terminology, one click apart.

💡

Ask the Right Questions

Before every oncologist visit, get a personalized list of questions based on the latest lab results, treatment stage, and any changes since the last appointment. Walk in prepared, not overwhelmed.

🔬

Find Clinical Trials You Qualify For

Automatically searches ClinicalTrials.gov and EU registries for trials matching your molecular profile, location, and treatment history. From your local hospital to Vienna, Barcelona, or Boston — nothing overlooked.

📖

A Second Opinion, Always Available

Cross-references your treatment with medical textbooks (DeVita), NCCN protocols, and latest research. Not to question your doctor — but to make sure the standard of care is being met and no option is missed.

📱

Everything in Your Pocket

Chat via WhatsApp anytime — ask about a lab value, check when the next cycle is, or get a quick summary. Open the dashboard for trends, timelines, and deep dives. It's there when you need it, wherever you are.

🔤

Medical ↔ Plain Language

Every medical term, abbreviation, and lab value explained in words you understand. Switch between plain language and clinical terminology with one click. Learn at your own pace — or just get the answer fast.

📈

See How Treatment Is Working

Visual trends of tumor markers, blood counts, and inflammation indices across every cycle. See the trajectory — is the treatment working? Are the numbers heading the right direction? No guessing.

👨‍👩‍👧

Keep Your Family Informed

Generate clear, compassionate treatment summaries for family members who want to help but don't know how to ask. Share via WhatsApp or print — no medical degree required to understand.

Your Data, Your Control

GDPR Compliant

All patient data is processed in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Stored in Your Google Drive

Your medical documents stay in your own Google Drive. Oncoteam reads and processes data but never stores originals outside your control. You can revoke access anytime.

Multi-Layer Security

Google OAuth, API authentication, and encrypted connections. No unauthorized access to patient data — ever.

Full Transparency

Every data access and AI decision is logged. You can see exactly what Oncoteam read, analyzed, and recommended — and why.

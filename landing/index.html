The Problem

Cancer patients and their families face an overwhelming flood of medical data — lab results, imaging reports, genetic tests, treatment protocols, clinical trial options. Making sense of it all while fighting the disease is an impossible burden.

Our Solution

Oncoteam is a persistent AI agent that works alongside you. It reads your medical documents, tracks your labs, monitors clinical trials, checks drug interactions, and prepares you for every oncologist visit — so you can focus on what matters.

What Oncoteam Does

📊

Treatment Dashboard

All your medical data in one place — labs, imaging, medications, treatment timeline. Bilingual interface (Slovak/English).

🔬

Lab Analysis

Automated lab result interpretation with reference ranges, trend analysis, inflammation indices (SII, Ne/Ly ratio), and pre-cycle safety checks.

🧬

Clinical Trial Matching

Searches ClinicalTrials.gov and PubMed for trials matching your molecular profile. Checks eligibility based on your biomarkers, prior treatments, and location.

🤖

Autonomous Agent

Runs autonomously to scan new documents, extract data, update your treatment timeline, and flag anything that needs attention — even when you're not looking.

📋

Protocol Tracking

Embedded clinical protocol with dose modification rules, cumulative dose thresholds, toxicity grading, and cycle delay criteria.

🌍

EU-Wide Trial Search

Monitors clinical trials across 14 European countries — from Vienna to Barcelona. Prioritized by travel distance, checked every 6 hours.

📱

Proactive WhatsApp Alerts

Safety alerts, weekly briefings, and trial updates delivered to your phone via WhatsApp — so nothing critical gets missed.

📖

Medical Dictionary

Built-in bilingual dictionary with professional and plain-language explanations of every medical term. Searchable from any page.

👁

Agent Observatory

Watch all 17 autonomous agents work in real time. See thinking steps, tool calls, costs, and run history. Full transparency into every AI decision.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Updates

Generate easy-to-understand treatment summaries for family members. Available in Slovak and English, shareable via WhatsApp or print.

🔗

MCP-Native Architecture

Built on the Model Context Protocol — open, interoperable, and designed to work with any AI assistant. Your data stays yours.

Privacy & Data Protection

GDPR Compliant

All patient data is processed in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Your Data, Your Control

Patient documents are stored in your own Google Drive. Oncoteam reads and processes data but never stores originals outside your control.

Controlled Access

Multi-layer authentication: Google OAuth, API bearer tokens, and MCP server authentication. No unauthorized access to patient data.

Full Audit Trail

Every data access, AI decision, and document interaction is logged. Complete transparency for patients and caregivers.
